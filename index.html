About Lehaa

LEHAA for Trading and Agriculture Investment has been established in 1998 as a result of Saudi investment in Egypt in the Agriculture sector, LEHAA's headquarter is located in Cairo city, the farms and Warehouses are located in Upper Egypt (Garf HusienAswan) nearest Naser Lake and there is a canal along the 1,750 km stretch of Lake Nasser to the main lifting station to the project.

LEHAA is currently looking for UK trading partners for their forthcoming Egyptian crop of table and processing potatoes which are available from February to July.
